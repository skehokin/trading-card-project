Cat

Meowing developed in domestic cats purely in order to communicate with humans. Adult wild cats do not meow.

  • Scientific Name: Felis Catus
  • Average Length: 53 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 12 years
  • Habitat: Deserts (formerly)

These small furry mammals are highly efficient predators, evolving from African Wildcats domesticated by humans perhaps as long ago as 7500 BC. They are now present on all continents aside from Antarctica.
